Why Munich?

Munich hosts the largest folk festival in the world!

Every September, Munich hosts the largest folk festival in the world. The city has several of the largest breweries in Germany and is famous for its beer and its annual Oktoberfest celebration. Here's how the tradition got started. Every year, millions of revelers descend on a tent-filled meadow in Munich to celebrate the love of long-ago Bavarian royals at a celebration known as Oktoberfest.

Munich, on the other hand, is not only wealthier but far better preserved. People from all over the world are drawn there for its economy, castles, and bustling street life. Munich is a major tourist destination and a convention centre. Book publishing and printing and television production are also important.

Ice Cream Shops

My favourite Ice cream shops in Munich

Ballabeni Icecream

This icecream shop is suggested for the good combination of tastes like basilico-lemon and other unusual ones. The quality of the ice cream is good and this is why normally you need to queue in front of it

Address:

Theresienstraße 46, 80333 München

What I like about it

Excellent icecream, interesting but delicious flavours. They also give you an additional spoon of one flavour you would like to try. When we were there all flavours were gluten free so that was a plus also.

Der Verrückte Eismaster

Creative and new ice cream flavors that you can hardly find anywhere else. Plus it's extra tasty and with friendly service. The tasting spoon makes you want more!

Address:

Amalienstraße 77, 80799 München

What I like about it

I like going there because there are always crazy new varieties that you can try. You always get a spoon to try. The waitresses are very nice and the prices are very good in relation to the quality.

IceDate

A vegan ice cream shop! Good choices of flavours and generous portions. Ice without added sugar that tastes. It tastes different than normal ice, don't expect the taste of normal non-vegan ice.

Address:

Amalienstraße 91, 80799 München

What I like about it

A must-go for ice cream lovers who want to enjoy healthy and delicious ice cream. The texture is probably a bit more dense than regular dairy ice cream, and two balls are certainly enough (for me at least!!).
